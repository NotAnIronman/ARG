Press & Media Information

For journalists, media organizations, and accreditation requests.

Media contact

For press inquiries, interview requests, and media partnerships, contact: press@deliaislnews.com

For urgent inquiries related to breaking coverage, contact the managing editor directly: c.hullery@deliaislnews.com

Press accreditation

Deli Aisle News issues press credentials on a case-by-case basis for events we are covering or hosting. To apply, submit your name, outlet, and a brief description of your coverage needs to press@deliaislnews.com.

Accreditation requests are reviewed by the managing editor. We reserve the right to deny accreditation at our discretion. Accreditation may be revoked if a credentialed journalist is found to have misrepresented their affiliation or purpose.

Note: accreditation requests submitted by individuals affiliated with investigative bodies, oversight offices, or any party with a direct interest in our reporting will not be approved. Such requests will be logged and reviewed by our legal counsel.

Embargoes & advance materials

Deli Aisle News occasionally provides embargoed materials to accredited journalists. Embargoes are lifted only when explicitly stated in writing. Violation of embargo terms may result in removal from our distribution list.

Note: Certain documents related to Case 7731-A are not eligible for embargo distribution due to ongoing review.

Republication

Requests to republish Deli Aisle News content should be directed to press@deliaislnews.com. Unauthorized republication of our reporting is not permitted.

Automated scraping of our site is monitored. Repeated scraping attempts originating from the same network will be logged.

About our coverage

Deli Aisle News is an independent digital newsroom. Our editorial decisions are made independently of our funding sources and are not subject to external direction. For questions about specific coverage decisions, contact our editorial standards page.

Note: We do not comment on inquiries regarding unpublished drafts, internal communications, or editorial decisions related to ongoing investigations.

Security & confidentiality

Deli Aisle News maintains strict confidentiality regarding sources, tip submissions, and internal communications. We do not disclose the identities of anonymous contributors or individuals who contact us through secure channels.

For secure submissions, use our encrypted tip form. Do not send sensitive information through standard email.
