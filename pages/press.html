Press & Media Information

For journalists, media organizations, and accreditation requests.

Media contact

For press inquiries, interview requests, and media partnerships, contact: press@deliaislnews.com

For urgent inquiries related to breaking coverage, contact the managing editor directly: c.hullery@deliaislnews.com

Press accreditation

Deli Aisle News issues press credentials on a case-by-case basis for events we are covering or hosting. To apply, submit your name, outlet, and a brief description of your coverage needs to press@deliaislnews.com.

Accreditation requests are reviewed by the managing editor. We reserve the right to deny accreditation at our discretion. Accreditation may be revoked if a credentialed journalist is found to have misrepresented their affiliation or purpose.

Note: accreditation requests submitted by individuals affiliated with law enforcement agencies, federal prosecutors' offices, or any party with a legal interest in our coverage will not be approved. Such requests will be logged and reviewed by our legal counsel.

Republication

Requests to republish Deli Aisle News content should be directed to press@deliaislnews.com. Unauthorized republication of our reporting is not permitted.

About our coverage

Deli Aisle News is an independent digital newsroom. Our editorial decisions are made independently of our funding sources and are not subject to external direction. For questions about specific coverage decisions, contact our editorial standards page.
