The Deli Aisle Network: A Public Timeline

Compiled by Voss Harrity | Updated 2 hours ago

2015 — August joins the Deli Aisle. August begins working within the Deli Aisle community, initially as a volunteer moderator. His organizational skills and consistency quickly make him a central figure.

2016–2018 — Growth and structure. The Deli Aisle expands from a casual chat space into a structured community hub. August formalizes roles, introduces daily activity logs, and establishes the first version of the Deli Aisle’s internal record‑keeping system.

April 3, 2011 — Gustau begins working at the Deli Aisle. Though earlier than August, Gustau’s role remains largely operational. His long tenure becomes a point of tension years later.

2019 — Administrative expansion. As membership grows, August introduces new administrative accounts and permissions. Several long‑standing accounts are retired. The identity behind one elevated account remains unconfirmed.

2020 — Internal restructuring. August reorganizes the Deli Aisle’s internal systems, citing “efficiency improvements.” Some members later describe this period as unusually secretive.

2021 — Increased scrutiny. Community members notice stricter enforcement of daily Wordle completion rules. August begins privately reviewing older activity logs. No public explanation is given.

March 2023 — Tensions rise. Several moderators, including Patricio Olivarez, express concerns about administrative decisions. Patricio leaves the Deli Aisle shortly afterward.

April 16, 2023 — The missed Wordle. August fails to complete the daily Wordle for the first time in years. Members note that he seemed distracted and unusually quiet that day.

April 17, 2023 — August steps down. In a sudden announcement, August resigns from his position. No detailed explanation is provided. On the same day, **Gustau is appointed Acting Owner**. Several internal systems are updated within hours of the transition.

April 18, 2023 — Attempted departure and arrest. August and his wife attempt to leave the country early in the morning. They are intercepted and August is taken into custody. Official statements describe the arrest as related to “Wordle evasion.”

April–June 2023 — Public confusion. Community members express disbelief at the severity of the charges. Rumors circulate about missing records, unusual administrative activity, and unexplained financial discrepancies. No official clarification is provided.

July 1, 2023 — Hacking attempts. The Deli Aisle News website experiences coordinated intrusion attempts targeting archived documents. The timing coincides with the morning August is found unresponsive in his cell.

July 1, 2023 — August’s death. August is discovered unresponsive in federal custody. Officials describe the death as an apparent suicide. Community members dispute the explanation, citing inconsistencies in the timeline and unanswered questions about his final days.

July 4, 2023 — Investigation begins. Public inquiries intensify. Conflicting statements, missing records, and unexplained gaps in the timeline lead to widespread speculation. The official investigation remains ongoing.
