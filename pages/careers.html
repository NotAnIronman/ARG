Records Management Specialist

Fort Worth, TX · Contract · Listing ID: DAN-2026-RMS

Deli Aisle News is seeking a contract Records Management Specialist for a defined-term engagement. This role involves the organization, cataloguing, secure handling, and in some cases physical retrieval of documentation related to ongoing editorial projects.

The successful candidate will have:

  • Experience handling sensitive or confidential documentation in a professional context
  • Familiarity with federal records systems or legal document management (preferred)
  • The ability to work independently with minimal oversight and without discussing the scope of their work with colleagues outside the immediate project team
  • A demonstrated history of discretion in professional environments
  • Availability to work non-standard hours, including occasional overnight shifts, as required by project timelines
  • No active affiliations with any federal law enforcement agency or contractor

Compensation is negotiated individually based on experience. This position is not listed with any external recruiting platform. Applications submitted through third-party job boards will not be reviewed.

To apply: use the secure tip form with subject reference: DAN-2026-RMS. Do not use the standard careers email for this listing.
