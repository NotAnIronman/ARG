Our Mission

At Deli Aisle News, we believe that information should be accessible, verifiable, and responsibly reported. Our team works around the clock to bring clarity to complex stories, provide context where it’s needed most, and ensure that our readers have the facts they need to stay informed.

We are committed to editorial independence. No outside organization, public official, or private entity dictates our coverage. Our newsroom operates under strict ethical guidelines designed to protect the integrity of our reporting.

Our History

Deli Aisle News began as a small community project created by August, who served as the organization’s first Editor-in-Chief. Under his leadership, the newsroom expanded its coverage, launched its investigative desk, and developed the digital infrastructure that powers the site today.

In April 2020, August stepped down from his position. Following his departure, longtime contributor Gustau assumed the role of Acting Editor. The transition marked a period of rapid restructuring, including updates to internal systems, workflow processes, and editorial review procedures.

Our Team

August — Founder & Former Editor-in-Chief

August founded Deli Aisle News with the goal of creating a space for honest, community-driven journalism. Known for his meticulous attention to detail and commitment to transparency, he oversaw the newsroom’s growth from a small project to a widely recognized publication.

His contributions continue to shape the organization’s values and editorial standards.

Gustau — Acting Editor

With nearly a decade of experience at Deli Aisle News, Gustau stepped into the role of Acting Editor following August’s departure. He is responsible for overseeing daily operations, managing editorial workflows, and ensuring that published content meets the organization’s standards.

Colleagues describe him as dedicated and highly structured, with a strong focus on procedural consistency.

Patricio Olivarez — Former Staff Writer

Patricio contributed to Deli Aisle News as a staff writer, covering community stories, local events, and internal features. Known for his thorough reporting and attention to detail, he played a key role in documenting the organization’s early growth.

Patricio left the newsroom prior to the recent events but remains a respected figure among former colleagues.

Editorial Standards

Our newsroom follows a rigorous multi-step review process to ensure accuracy and fairness. All articles undergo fact-checking, source verification, and editorial oversight before publication. We maintain a strict separation between our reporting and any external influence.

Readers who identify errors or inconsistencies are encouraged to contact our corrections team. Transparency is central to our mission.
