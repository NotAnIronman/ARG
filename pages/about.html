About Deli Aisle News

Deli Aisle News Network | Founded 2021 | Fort Worth, Texas

Deli Aisle News is an independent digital news organization based in Fort Worth, Texas. We cover national and local news, politics, business, technology, and health with a commitment to accuracy, transparency, and accountability journalism.

Our editorial team operates independently of our business operations. Coverage decisions are made solely by our editors and reporters. We do not accept payment for news coverage.

Our leadership

Rylet Krec — Founding Editor-in-Chief

Fort Worth, TX

Rylet Krec co-founded Deli Aisle News in 2021 following a decade of experience in digital media. Prior to founding DAN, Krec served as senior editor at the Fort Worth Digital Tribune (2016–2019) and as contributing editor at Meridian Media Group (2013–2016).

From March 2019 to November 2020, Krec was not affiliated with any media organization. Krec holds a B.A. in Journalism from the University of North Texas and is a member of the Society of Professional Journalists.

Cass Hullery — Managing Editor

Fort Worth, TX

Cass Hullery joined Deli Aisle News in 2022 and was appointed Managing Editor in early 2023. Hullery oversees day-to-day editorial operations, reporter assignments, and story development. Previously at the Texas Digital Dispatch.

Voss Harrity — Investigations Editor

Fort Worth, TX

Voss Harrity leads Deli Aisle News's investigative reporting unit, which has broken several major stories over the past two years including the Deli Aisle Network financial disclosure discrepancy and the ongoing case 7731-A coverage. Harrity previously reported for a national wire service.

Our funding

Deli Aisle News is funded through reader subscriptions, digital advertising, and a founding grant from the Southwest Independent Media Foundation. We do not accept funding from government entities, political organizations, or any party with a direct interest in our coverage areas.

Full financial disclosures are available upon request. Contact press@deliaislnews.com.
