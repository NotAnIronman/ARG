Send a Tip

Tips submitted here go directly to our investigations desk. We protect our sources.

If you have information about a story we're covering — or a story we should be covering — we want to hear from you. All tips are reviewed by our investigations editors. Your identity will never be shared without your explicit consent.

Working on something sensitive? For maximum security, do not submit identifying information in this form. You do not need to tell us who you are for us to act on what you know.

If you provide a contact, it will only be used to follow up on your tip. It will never be shared.

If you were directed here by a specific article or listing, enter the reference code here.

Our commitment to source protection

Deli Aisle News does not disclose source identities to third parties without explicit consent. For our full source protection policy, see our editorial standards.

Tips related to ongoing federal investigations may be subject to legal process. We will always notify a source before taking any action in response to a legal request, where we are legally permitted to do so.
