Corrections

Deli Aisle News corrects errors of fact promptly. This log is updated as corrections are issued.

CORRECTION ISSUED — October 2, 2026

Article: "Former owner of Deli Aisle found dead in Federal prison" (published October 1, 2026)
Correction: An earlier version of this article stated that August was held at MCC Houston "without legal representation" at the time of his death. This was incorrect. He was represented by counsel throughout his detention. The article has been updated. We regret the error.

CORRECTION ISSUED — September 30, 2026

Article: "Bartholemew and Saintiagoon issue conflicting statements" (published September 29, 2026)
Correction: An earlier version misstated the time of Saintiagoon's statement as "mid-afternoon." The statement was given at approximately 6:40 p.m. The article has been updated.

NOTE — Entry removed September 15, 2026

One correction entry from this log was removed on September 15, 2026. The removal was made at the request of our legal counsel and described as "premature." A note has been placed in this log to acknowledge the removal in the interest of transparency. The entry concerned a characterization of a named official's involvement in events described in a prior article. We expect to revisit this entry when circumstances permit.
