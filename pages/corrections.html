Corrections

Deli Aisle News corrects errors of fact promptly. This log is updated as corrections are issued.

CORRECTION ISSUED — July 3, 2023

Article: "Former owner of Deli Aisle found unresponsive in federal custody" (published July 1, 2023)
Correction: An earlier version of this article stated that August had “not requested access to any internal records” prior to his arrest. This was incorrect. Publicly released documents confirm that August submitted a request for archived transaction logs on April 17. The article has been updated. We regret the error.

CORRECTION ISSUED — June 28, 2023

Article: "Officials address discrepancies in Deli Aisle timeline" (published June 27, 2023)
Correction: An earlier version misstated the time of the administrative system update as “shortly after midnight.” The update occurred at 03:14 CST on April 18. The article has been updated.

NOTE — Entry removed June 12, 2023

One correction entry from this log was removed on June 12, 2023. The removal was made at the request of our legal counsel and described as “premature.” A note has been placed in this log to acknowledge the removal in the interest of transparency. The entry concerned a mischaracterization of an individual’s role in the administrative restructuring that occurred on April 17. We expect to revisit this entry when circumstances permit.

CORRECTION ISSUED — May 19, 2023

Article: "Membership revenue breakdown released" (published May 18, 2023)
Correction: The article previously stated that all financial entries were “fully categorized.” This was incorrect. One entry labeled “adjustments” was later determined to include multiple sub‑entries not visible in the initial disclosure. The article has been updated.

CORRECTION ISSUED — April 22, 2023

Article: "Leadership transition at the Deli Aisle Network" (published April 18, 2023)
Correction: An earlier version stated that the transition occurred “without changes to administrative permissions.” This was incorrect. Internal logs confirm that several permission tiers were modified on April 18. The article has been updated.

To report a factual error, contact corrections@deliaislnews.com or use the contact form.
